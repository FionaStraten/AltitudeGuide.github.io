Lake Louise Score

The Lake Louise Score is a tool for diagnosing the Acute Mountain Sickness (AMS) and assessing its severity. It is based on self-assessment and does not take external/environmental paramters into account so far. The diagnostic criteria, the scoring system for symptoms and signs of Actue Mountain Sickness and the resulting recommendations were designed by the Lake Louise AMS Scoring Consensus Committee.
